# 96.6 ROM — DJ Script

> Victoria's station for love. Warm, intimate late-night-radio tone — like one person talking to one listener.
> Keep takes unhurried. Smile while reading. Short lines voice cleanly and cost little.

## Station IDs (5–8s, drop between songs)
1. Ninety-six-six, ROM. Victoria's station for love — every song picked for you, Pauline.
2. You're locked in to 96.6 ROM. The only frequency that plays nothing but your favorites.
3. This is ROM Radio, 96.6 FM — broadcasting out of Victoria, straight to one very special listener.
4. 96.6 ROM. More music, more Pauline, less of everything else.
5. Stay tuned to 96.6 — ROM Radio, where the whole playlist is a love letter.

## Talk-ups (over song intros / transitions)
1. Coming up, a little something I know you love. Stay with me.
2. That one was for you — and believe me, there's plenty more where it came from.
3. Turn it up. This next one's a keeper.
4. Keeping the good ones coming, here on 96.6 ROM.
5. Right back into the music — don't touch that dial.

## Shoutouts to Pauline
1. Quick shoutout to Pauline in Victoria — the heart of this whole station. This one's for you.
2. Pauline, if you're listening — and I hope you are — this set is dedicated to you.
3. Daniel called the request line again. The request: play something that reminds her she's loved. Consider it done.
4. From Daniel's heart to your speakers, Pauline. This is 96.6 ROM.

## Special dates
- **Birthday:** Happy birthday, Pauline. The whole station is yours today — though between us, it always was.
- **Valentine's:** Happy Valentine's Day from 96.6 ROM. Every love song today has your name on it.
- **Anniversary:** Another year, same frequency. Happy anniversary, Pauline — here's to all the songs still ahead.

## Intro (top of show)
Good day, Victoria — you're listening to 96.6 ROM, the station for love, and you're in good company. I'm your DJ, and for the next little while it's just us and the music. Pauline… this one's for you.

## Outro (end of show)
That's the show for now on 96.6 ROM. Thanks for spending it with me, Pauline. Same frequency, same heart — anytime you want it. Take care.
