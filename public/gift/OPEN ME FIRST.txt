HOW TO OPEN THE RADIO (first time only)
────────────────────────────────────────────

Because this is a personal app made just for you (not from the App Store),
macOS shows a one-time "Apple could not verify…" warning. It is completely
safe. Here is how to open it — you only do this ONCE:

EASIEST WAY
  1. Right-click (or Control-click) the "96.6 ROM" app
  2. Choose "Open"
  3. Click "Open" again in the little box

IF YOU DON'T SEE AN "OPEN" OPTION (newer Macs)
  1. Double-click "96.6 ROM" once — you'll get the warning. Click "Done".
  2. Open  System Settings ▸ Privacy & Security
  3. Scroll down — you'll see a line about "96.6 ROM" being blocked.
     Click "Open Anyway", then "Open".

That's it. From then on, just double-click it like any app.
You can also drag it into your Dock to keep it handy.

When it opens: type your name to tune in, press play, and enjoy.
The whole station is yours.  — D
